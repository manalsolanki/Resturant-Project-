About Us

Since our modest beginnings in 2020 with a little space in Toronto’s stylish Humber locale, ‘The Holy Epic Steakhouse’ was alwyas glad to have more and more of satisfied customers. We are a group of culinary experts and steak lovers who gathered from many countries around the world to open this one-of-a-kind restaurant in Canada. What defines our restaurant is to offer steak meals in several flavors in order to keep in line with the multiple cultures that exist in Canada. The restaurant is also pleased to offer its wonderful meals to tourists from all over the world to satisfy all tastes.

Our steakhouse provides the best steak in town. We serve steaks inspired by chinese cuisine on the menu.
